Why are the Glaciers melting

The main reason the ice caps are melting thanks to human activities. With the primary culprit being the industrial revolution. According to Worldwildlife.org, glaciers are rapidly meliting due to carbon dioxide and other greenhouse emissions raising temperatures.

What can we do

  1. Use electricity and water wisely.
  2. Swap from energy produced by fossil fuels to clean energy sources such as wind and solar energy.
  3. Drive less and walk more
  4. Use hybrid engines instead of combustion engines
  5. Build sea walls to block warm water
